### Frequently asked questions

If you have anything else you want to ask, [reach out to us](mailto:hello@getworthit.com).

[What is a net worth tracker](https://getworthit.com/#)

It adds up your assets and subtracts your debts to show a single number. You also see monthly change to spot trends. This helps you decide where to save or pay down. Worth It keeps the process simple and fast.

[Do I need to connect my bank](https://getworthit.com/#)

No. Worth It is manual first so you stay in control. Enter balances or paste totals from your bank or broker. Many users prefer this for accuracy and privacy. You choose when to update.

[What can I track](https://getworthit.com/#)

Bank accounts, cash, investments, pensions, real estate, vehicles, and debts. Add custom assets for anything else. Multi currency with daily rates is built in. Notes and simple tags help you stay organized.

[How often should I update](https://getworthit.com/#)

Update once a month for a clean signal and less noise. Weekly is fine if you want finer movement. Set a reminder inside the app. The graph highlights true progress over time.

[Can I export my data](https://getworthit.com/#)

Yes. Export to CSV for Excel or Google Sheets at any time. Keep personal backups with your files. You can re import later using the same columns. Your data remains portable.

[How is my data kept private](https://getworthit.com/#)

Your data syncs securely across devices. You can delete it from within the app at any time. Protect access with Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode. We respect your choice to stay manual.
